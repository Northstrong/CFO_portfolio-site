Franck Muller: The Master of Complications

Published: August 2, 2025

Franck Muller has earned its title as “Master of Complications” for good reason. Founded in Geneva in the 1990s, the brand quickly rose to fame thanks to its bold case designs and jaw-dropping mechanical features.

With creations like the Crazy Hours, Aeternitas Mega, and Vanguard series, Franck Muller broke all rules of classic design while preserving technical mastery.

It’s a favorite among trendsetters and collectors who crave conversation-starters and boundary-pushing design.
