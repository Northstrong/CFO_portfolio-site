About CFO Luxurious Timepieces

CFO Luxurious Timepieces is a trusted curator and reseller of high-end, authenticated luxury watches from the world’s most prestigious brands — including Rolex, Audemars Piguet, Patek Philippe, and Richard Mille. We are not just in the business of timekeeping — we deal in timeless value.

Our name stands for: Chronus Fides Opulentia — Time. Trust. Luxury. Every timepiece we offer reflects this philosophy. We deliver only verified authentic watches, carefully sourced for collectors, professionals, and elite lifestyle clients across Nigeria and beyond.

Whether you’re buying your first investment piece or selling a rare model, we combine luxury taste with absolute trust. With discreet service, secure transactions, and exclusive selections, CFO is your gateway to horological excellence.
